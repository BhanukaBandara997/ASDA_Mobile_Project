How Do Points Work?
Member Levels
The first member level upgrade will occur on Dec 8, 2017 PT. Your Member Level is based on the number of reward points you have accumulated over the past 12 months, we calculate the points on the last day of each month. You can reach higher levels by collecting the number of points required by shopping, interacting and just visiting daily, and new points and level will be effective on 8th of next month. Remember that you must collect enough points each month to avoid dropping down a level!
Shopping Points
Earn 5 points for each day that you buy items above or equal to US $2 (max 5 points per day)
You can earn bonus points by shopping in multiple categories each day (up to 5 points per day)
